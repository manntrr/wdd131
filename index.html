About Me

My name is Tracy Mann and I go by Tracy. I was born in Hornell, N.Y., USA. I have moved around a lot in my youth, living in North Dakota, Minnesota and Western New York State. I joined the Army out of high school and served in Fort Knox, Kentucky, West Texas, and Augsburg, Germany. I returned to Western New York for school after leaving the Army, and got a job working in a Retail Grocery Store Help Desk in Retail support. I have worked my way through the company into an Engineering role, now supporting Advertising Technology and Marketing Technology. The company I work for is called Ahold/Delhaize. My company had moved me to Greenville, South Carolina in 2001. A few years later I moved to Simpsonville, South Carolina, a close suburb of Greenville and I have been here since. I am sealed to my wife Haydee, and we have no children at this time. We love to visit her family in Peru when we can and see other sites as well.
